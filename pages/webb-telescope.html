Background

As part of the Web and Mobile App Design and Development (WMDD) program at Langara College, I undertook the challenge of revitalizing the Webb Telescope's website. Despite the telescope's technological marvel, its existing website fell short in reflecting its sophistication, lacking dynamism and a modern aesthetic.

Problem

The existing website failed to convey the advanced technology embodied by the Webb Telescope. Its outdated design lacked the dynamism needed to engage users and present the telescope in a contemporary light.

Project Goals

  • Update UI Kit: Implement a refreshed UI Kit to modernize the visual elements of the website.
  • Concept Integration: Introduce a concept that aligns seamlessly with the subject, enhancing the overall user experience.
  • Homepage Redesign: Transform the homepage to be more engaging and reflective of the cutting-edge technology represented by the Webb Telescope.
  • Gallery Page Redesign: Overhaul the gallery page to showcase the telescope's stunning images in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner.

Solution

The integration of a concept that resonates with the telescope's advanced nature added a layer of sophistication. The homepage and gallery page were both redesigned to provide a more engaging and visually pleasing user experience.

Reflections & Take-aways

The importance of developing a unique and consistent concept became evident throughout the project. By exploring various ideas and implementing them consistently across the website, a truly distinctive and modernized product emerged. The project underscored the significance of idea exploration in the design process, resulting in a website that now effectively showcases the advanced technology embodied by the Webb Telescope.
